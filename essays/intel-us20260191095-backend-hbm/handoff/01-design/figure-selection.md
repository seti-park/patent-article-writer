## Cover candidate (5:2 header) — surfaced for owner override

- **Recommended: FIG. 1B** — the isometric 8-high die stack on a base die. Highest instant force in a feed card ("a new kind of tall memory chip"), it depicts the claimed core (a memory die stack, "8-high and beyond," on a base die, claim 1 + `[0023]`), and its six numerals (110-115) fit the cover-caption numeral budget (≤ 6, SURF-003).
- **Alternate A: FIG. 1A** — logic die (106) beside the memory stack (104) on one package: the single best thesis-in-one-image (logic + memory on one flow). Not recommended AS the cover only because its seven labeled numerals (100-106) exceed the cover-caption budget; strong if the orchestrator prefers the thesis literalized over the tower. Selected as a body figure regardless.
- **Alternate B: FIG. 1F** — the most literal depiction of the CLAIMED NOVELTY (back-end "TRANSISTOR" layers). Not recommended as cover: dense at 5:2 scale for a lay feed. Selected as a body figure.
