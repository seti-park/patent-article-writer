# Example

[Post 1]
Every wound battery cell carries the same homework: the current generated inside the electrode stack has to reach the terminals outside the can. So far the industry has known two ways to do it. Attach a small part called a tab to the foil, or go tabless, the category Tesla made famous at Battery Day 2020. On today's tabless lines, lasers slit the bare foil edge into flags before winding.

Tesla's next tabless filing doesn't cut the foil into flags. It folds the edge, and the fold is the tab.

[Post 2]
The filing is US 2026/0196678 A1, "Hemmed Tabless Electrodes for Energy Storage Devices, and Methods Thereof." The architecture is simple. An electrode film sits on a foil that keeps a bare edge at one end. That edge is hemmed, folded back on itself like the hem of a trouser leg, until the fold stands above the film. When the hemmed electrodes are wound with separators into a jellyroll, one winding of the hemmed edge touches the next, and the folds stack into a continuous folded rim. The collector attaches to that rim as both the electrical contact and the mechanical attachment. No tab is welded on. Nothing is cut away. Neither add a tab nor subtract a flag. Only fold.

[Post 3]
The sharpest paragraph in Tesla's hemmed-electrode filing is not a claim chart. It is an equipment list: tabbing, slitting, notching, flag-interleaving, and debris-handling devices are all named as machines a hemmed line "may not necessarily require." The same fold also changes what happens inside the cell. Current collects along the whole folded rim instead of at one tab, the electron's travel gets shorter, and the document says internal electrical resistance can improve. That is the same advantage Tesla presented for tabless at Battery Day 2020. And because the hems need no slits, the document says they can increase the electrode's structural integrity and mitigate bending and tearing damage under tensile loads. One boundary worth naming: the filing never mentions the 4680 cell, Tesla's own 2019 tabless patent, or any production line. Reading its device list as the bill of machines a flag-cutting line would no longer need is the essay's mapping, standing on the document's own words.

[Post 4]
Companies announce tabless when it is convenient. They file when the edge is already being redesigned on paper. Whether Tesla swaps its own production process is outside the document. The things to watch are the grant, continuation filings that keep the hem-without-slits core, and production-line changes that drop the cutting stack the list names. US 2026/0196678 A1 is a pending application, filed 6 January 2025 and published 9 July 2026. Where the claims' requirements end and the description's menu of shapes begins is in the article.
